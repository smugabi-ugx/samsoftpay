How Subscriptions Work

For merchants: Create a plan with a name, amount, and interval. Share the subscribe link on your TikTok, website, or WhatsApp. Customers subscribe once and are billed automatically.

Billing: The worker charges subscribers at each interval. Failed charges set the subscription to failed. You can pause or cancel any subscription from the subscribers list.

For customers: They enter their phone number on the subscribe page. The first charge fires immediately. Subsequent charges happen automatically every week/month/year.

API: Create plans and subscriptions programmatically via POST /v1/subscription-plans and integrate into your app. See API docs.
