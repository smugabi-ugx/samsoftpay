How Customers Redeem

At checkout, customers enter the gift card code (e.g. SAMF-X4K2-9WQP) in the voucher field. The balance is deducted automatically. Partial redemption supported.

MTN Gift Card Redemption

Gift cards can be redeemed directly to MTN MoMo. Click the QR button on any card — customers scan the QR with their MTN MoMo app to receive the balance as mobile money.
